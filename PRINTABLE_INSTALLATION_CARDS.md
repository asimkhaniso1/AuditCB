## Printing Instructions

### For Business Cards (85mm x 55mm):
1. Print on 300gsm cardstock
2. Use the QR Code card design
3. Print double-sided with contact info on back
4. Laminate for durability

### For A5 Handouts (148mm x 210mm):
1. Print the platform-specific cards
2. Use 120gsm paper
3. Print in color for best results
4. Optional: laminate for field use

### For A4 Posters (210mm x 297mm):
1. Print the Benefits Poster
2. Use glossy paper for professional look
3. Mount on wall near audit prep area
4. Update QR codes monthly
