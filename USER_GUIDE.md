# AuditCB360 - User Guide

## 1. Getting Started

### Login
1.  Access the application URL.
2.  Enter your email and password.
3.  **Note**: If you are a new user, you must be approved by an Admin before logging in.

### Dashboard
Your dashboard displays:
-   **Upcoming Audits**: Plans assigned to you.
-   **Compliance**: Your qualification status and next witness audit due date.

## 2. Conducting an Audit

### 2.1 View Audit Plan
1.  Navigate to **Dashboard**.
2.  Click on a scheduled audit card or go to **Audit Planning**.
3.  Review the Audit Plan (Scope, Standard, Team).

### 2.2 Execution & Findings
1.  Go to **Audit Execution**.
2.  Select the active audit.
3.  **Checklists**: Go through the ISO checklist items.
    -   Mark as **Conform**, **NC (Major/Minor)**, or **OFI**.
    -   Add comments/evidence for every finding.
4.  **Raised Findings**: Major/Minor NCs are automatically tracked.

### 2.3 Report Generation
1.  Once all checklist items are complete, go to **Reporting**.
2.  Click **Generate Report**.
3.  Review the Executive Summary.
4.  **Download PDF**: Click the PDF icon to generating a branded audit report.

## 3. Offline Mode
The app works offline!
-   **No Internet?** You can still open the app, view checklists, and save data.
-   **Sync**: When you reconnect, data will automatically sync without loss.
-   Look for the **OFFLINE** indicator in the header.

## 4. Support
For technical issues, contact the System Administrator at `admin@auditcb360.com`.
